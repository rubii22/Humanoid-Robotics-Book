# References - Physical AI & Humanoid Robotics

## Academic References

### Core Textbooks and References

1. Siciliano, B., & Khatib, O. (2016). *Springer Handbook of Robotics* (2nd ed.). Springer.
   - Comprehensive reference covering all aspects of robotics including humanoid robotics.

2. Khatib, O., Park, H. J., & Bjoerkman, M. (Eds.). (2023). *Humanoid Robotics: A Reference*. Springer.
   - Detailed reference specifically for humanoid robotics research and development.

3. Thrun, S., Burgard, W., & Fox, D. (2005). *Probabilistic Robotics*. MIT Press.
   - Essential reference for robot perception, localization, and mapping.

4. Spong, M. W., Hutchinson, S., & Vidyasagar, M. (2020). *Robot Modeling and Control* (2nd ed.). Wiley.
   - Fundamental text on robot kinematics, dynamics, and control theory.

5. Murray, R. M., Li, Z., & Sastry, S. S. (2017). *A Mathematical Introduction to Robotic Manipulation*. CRC Press.
   - Mathematical foundations for robotic manipulation and control.

### ROS 2 and Middleware

6. Kammerl, J., et al. (2022). *ROS 2 for Robotics: A Comprehensive Guide to Robot Operating System*.
   - Official ROS 2 documentation and best practices for robotics applications.

7. Quigley, M., et al. (2009). "ROS: an open-source Robot Operating System." *ICRA Workshop on Open Source Software*.
   - Original ROS paper providing foundational concepts extended in ROS 2.

### Computer Vision and Perception

8. Szeliski, R. (2022). *Computer Vision: Algorithms and Applications* (2nd ed.). Springer.
   - Comprehensive reference for computer vision algorithms used in robotics.

9. Hartley, R., & Zisserman, A. (2004). *Multiple View Geometry in Computer Vision* (2nd ed.). Cambridge University Press.
   - Essential reference for 3D computer vision and geometric transformations.

10. Goodfellow, I., Bengio, Y., & Courville, A. (2016). *Deep Learning*. MIT Press.
    - Foundational text for deep learning applications in robotics.

### Humanoid Robotics Research

11. Kajita, S., Kanehiro, F., Kaneko, K., Fujiwara, K., & Harada, K. (2003). "Biped walking pattern generation by using preview control of zero-moment point." *Proceedings 2003 IEEE/RSJ International Conference on Intelligent Robots and Systems*.
    - Foundational paper on bipedal walking control for humanoid robots.

12. Kuffner, J. J., & LaValle, S. M. (2000). "RRT-connect: An efficient approach to single-query path planning." *Proceedings of ICRA*.
    - RRT algorithm widely used in humanoid robot navigation and motion planning.

13. Pratt, J., & Walking, I. M. (2006). "Virtual model control: A framework for compliant robotic control." *Proceedings of the ASME International Mechanical Engineering Congress and Exposition*.
    - Control framework for compliant humanoid robot systems.

### Vision-Language-Action Research

14. Radford, A., et al. (2021). "Learning transferable visual models from natural language supervision." *International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML)*.
    - CLIP model for vision-language understanding.

15. Chen, C., et al. (2022). "Language models as zero-shot planners: Extracting actionable knowledge for embodied agents." *International Conference on Machine Learning (ICML)*.
    - Foundation for language model-based robot planning.

16. Brohan, C., et al. (2022). "RT-1: Robotics transformer for real-world control at scale." *arXiv preprint arXiv:2208.01173*.
    - Vision-language-action models for robot control.
